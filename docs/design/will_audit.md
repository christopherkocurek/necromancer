# Will Audit: Current Abilities vs Third Age Precedents

## Audit Questions Applied to Each Ability

---

### N:100 Curse Breaking
*Remove curses when taking off items*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Theoden freed from Saruman's spell. Breaking enchantments.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Mental freedom.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Cursed items are a trap - freedom to unequip helps.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Essential utility for item management.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Important utility, clear precedent.

---

### N:101 Channeling
*Recognize staves/horns, use twice as efficiently*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Gandalf with his staff. Device mastery.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Understanding magical items.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None direct.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Device-focused builds.

**VERDICT: MODIFY** - Could move to Lore, or rename to emphasize willpower aspect ("Force of Will" - powering devices through mental strength).

---

### N:102 Strength in Adversity
*+1/+3 Str/Dex/Gra at 50%/25% health*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Sam carrying Frodo, desperate courage when wounded.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Fighting harder when desperate.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Survival when stealth fails.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Rewards risky play.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Classic "last stand" ability, good precedent.

---

### N:103 Formidable
*Kills scare enemies, injuries don't encourage them*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Aragorn's presence, warrior reputation.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Intimidation through prowess.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: After breaking stealth, enemies fear you.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Morale warfare.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Good presence ability.

---

### N:104 Inner Light
*+2 light intensity*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Gandalf's light, Galadriel's radiance. But those are magical beings.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Borderline - mortals don't glow.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Anti-stealth! More light = more visible.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Enables light-based builds.

**VERDICT: MOVE TO LORE** - Better fit with Lore's "Inner Light" ability that's already there. Remove duplicate.

---

### N:105 Indomitable
*Resist fear, confusion, stunning, hallucination. Slow hunger.*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Eowyn facing Witch-King. Mental fortitude.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Iron will.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Stay calm when hiding from terrifying enemies.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Core defensive ability.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Essential Will ability, excellent precedent.

---

### N:106 Oath
*Swear oath for rewards*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Oaths are HUGE in Tolkien. Feanor's oath, oath of the Dead.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Oaths have power.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None direct.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Major commitment mechanic!

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Very Tolkien, interesting mechanics.

---

### N:107 Poison Resistance
*Resist poison*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Hardy constitution, Aragorn's endurance.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Physical resistance.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Survive poison traps.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Defensive utility.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Simple but useful.

---

### N:108 Vengeance
*Extra damage die after being damaged in melee*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Fighting harder when hurt, righteous anger.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Pain fuels determination.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: After ambush fails, fight harder.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Rewards taking hits.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Good synergy with Strength in Adversity.

---

### N:109 Majesty
*Lower enemy morale by half Will difference*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Aragorn's kingly presence, commanding aura.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Leadership presence.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Enemies more likely to flee = easier to re-stealth.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Morale warfare capstone.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Excellent leadership ability.

---

### N:110 Constitution
*+1 Con*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: N/A - stat boost.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Capstone reward.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Standard capstone.

---

## Summary Recommendations

### KEEP (No Changes)
- Curse Breaking (0)
- Strength in Adversity (2)
- Formidable (3)
- Indomitable (5)
- Oath (6)
- Poison Resistance (7)
- Vengeance (8)
- Majesty (9)
- Constitution (10)

### MODIFY
- Channeling (1) -> Rename to "Force of Will" - emphasize mental power over devices

### REMOVE/MOVE
- Inner Light (4) -> Already exists in Lore tree, remove duplicate

### ADD (to fill slot)
- **"Defy Death"** - When reduced to 0 HP, Will save to survive with 1 HP (once per floor). Boromir's last stand, fighting on despite mortal wounds.

---

## Will Tree Assessment

Strong identity around mental fortitude, leadership, and endurance. Only issues:
- Inner Light duplicates Lore ability
- Channeling name doesn't emphasize Will aspect

Core fantasy: "I will not break. I will not fall. I will endure."
