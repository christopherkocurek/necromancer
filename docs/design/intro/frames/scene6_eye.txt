================================================================================
                    SCENE 6: THE LIDLESS EYE
                    Duration: 4 seconds (40 frames @ 10 FPS)
================================================================================

DESCRIPTION:
Camera pulls back rapidly. Screen goes dark. Beat of silence.
Then: The Eye opens. Massive ASCII eye fills the screen.
Vertical slit pupil, flames surrounding it. IT SEES YOU.
Hold for dramatic effect.

THIS IS THE CLIMAX OF THE INTRO.

================================================================================
                         FRAME SEQUENCE
================================================================================

FRAMES 1-5: Rapid pullback (zoom out effect)
FRAMES 6-10: Pure black (silence, tension)
FRAMES 11-20: Eye OPENS (the reveal)
FRAMES 21-40: Eye fully open, flames flicker (hold)

================================================================================
                    FRAMES 1-5: PULLBACK
================================================================================

Quick frames showing "camera" pulling back from prison pit:

FRAME 1: Close on Thráin (from previous scene)
FRAME 2: Pit view, smaller
FRAME 3: Dungeon corridor
FRAME 4: Fortress exterior (tiny)
FRAME 5: Just a dot of light, then BLACK

================================================================================
                    FRAMES 6-10: THE PAUSE
================================================================================

Pure black screen. 500ms of nothing.

This is critical for dramatic effect.
The player wonders: "Is that it? Is it over?"

Then...

================================================================================
                    FRAMES 21-40: THE HOLD
================================================================================

Eye stays fully open. Flames animate (cycle through 4 patterns).

FLAME CYCLE:
- Frame 21: Pattern A (flames up)
- Frame 22: Pattern B (flames angled)
- Frame 23: Pattern C (flames spread)
- Frame 24: Pattern D (flames settle)
- Frame 25: Pattern A (repeat)
- ... continues

The pupil (|) can pulse:
- Odd frames: single line |
- Even frames: double line ||

Creates sense of the Eye FOCUSING on viewer.

================================================================================
                         COLOR SPECIFICATION
================================================================================

FLAMES (*^~):
Cycle through:
- TERM_RED (4)
- TERM_ORANGE (3)
- TERM_YELLOW (11)
- TERM_ORANGE (3)
- (repeat)

EYE STRUCTURE (.-':):
- TERM_RED (4) - the eye itself is fiery

PUPIL (|):
- TERM_DARK (0) or TERM_L_DARK (8)
- Pure darkness - the void within

BACKGROUND:
- TERM_DARK (0) - absolute black

================================================================================
                         SOUND DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

If sound is ever added:
- Frames 1-5: Rushing wind (pullback)
- Frames 6-10: Silence (deafening)
- Frame 11: Low rumble begins
- Frame 19: BOOM (flames ignite)
- Frames 21+: Crackling fire, heartbeat

================================================================================
                         THE PSYCHOLOGICAL EFFECT
================================================================================

This scene should make the player feel:
1. The terror of being SEEN by ultimate evil
2. The power of Sauron, represented by his symbol
3. "Oh no, what have I gotten into?"
4. Excitement to face this challenge

The Eye doesn't blink. It doesn't look away. IT SEES YOU.
