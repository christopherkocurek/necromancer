# Melee Audit: Current Abilities vs Third Age Precedents

## Audit Questions Applied to Each Ability

---

### N:0 Power
*+1 damage sides, harder crits*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Strong warriors hit harder - Boromir, Gimli. Generic but valid.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Extra damage sides are incremental.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None. Pure combat ability.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Trade-off with crits creates decision. Good design.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Solid foundation ability with meaningful trade-off.

---

### N:1 Finesse
*Crit base 7->5*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Skilled swordsmen like Aragorn finding weak points.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Better technique, not supernatural.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None directly, but crits synergize with Assassination.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Pairs with Power for branching paths.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Good complement to Power, valid precedent.

---

### N:2 Knock Back
*Push enemies based on Str vs Con*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Shield work, Boromir driving back orcs.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Physical strength application.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Could enable repositioning after stealth attack.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Tactical positioning option.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Solid utility with tactical depth.

---

### N:3 Polearm Mastery
*+2 attack, set to receive*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Spear formations, guards with polearms.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Weapon specialization.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Set to receive could work from ambush.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Weapon-specific investment decision.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Good weapon specialization.

---

### N:4 Charge
*+3 Str/Dex after moving toward enemy*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Cavalry charges, Theoden's death charge, infantry rushes.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Momentum advantage.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Could synergize - charge from hidden position.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Rewards aggressive positioning.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Strong thematic fit, good gameplay.

---

### N:5 Follow-Through
*Continue attack after kill*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Aragorn clearing through orcs in Moria/Amon Hen.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Skilled warrior pressing advantage.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Rewards killing blows, positioning matters.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Classic ability with good precedent.

---

### N:6 Impale
*Attack through enemies with polearm/greatsword*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Long weapons reaching past front rank. Less clear.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Borderline. Hitting two enemies feels powerful.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Weapon-specific tactical option.

**VERDICT: MODIFY** - Keep but ensure it's weapon-gated properly.

---

### N:7 Subtlety
*Better crits with one hand free*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Aragorn often fights sword-only. Ranger style.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Technique over brute force.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Light, mobile fighting fits stealth approach!
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Trade shield protection for crit chance.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Excellent Ranger-style ability, stealth-compatible.

---

### N:8 Whirlwind Attack
*Free attack on all adjacent enemies*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: WEAK. No one character attacks 8 enemies at once.
2. **POWER SCALE**: TOO HIGH. This is First Age Fingolfin territory.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Anti-stealth - you're surrounded.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Powerful but situational.

**VERDICT: MODIFY** - Reduce scope. Maybe "up to 3 adjacent" or require specific weapon type.

---

### N:9 Zone of Control
*Free attack when enemy moves between adjacent squares*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Skilled warrior controlling space. Dueling technique.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Defensive positioning.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None direct, but tactical.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Positioning mastery reward.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Good tactical ability.

---

### N:10 Smite
*Maximum damage first attack, lose turn to recover*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Desperate powerful blows. Boromir's last stand.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Borderline. "Maximum possible damage" feels mythic.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Could be amazing opener from stealth.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: High risk/reward, turn economy trade.

**VERDICT: MODIFY** - Rename to "Mighty Blow" and tone down to "+Str damage" rather than "maximum". Add stealth synergy: even bigger bonus from hidden.

---

### N:11 Two Weapon Fighting
*Off-hand attack at -3 Str/Dex*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: WEAK. No notable dual-wielders in Third Age. Drizzt is D&D.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded mechanically.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Trade defense for offense.

**VERDICT: REMOVE or MODIFY** - Not Tolkien. Could replace with "Sword and Torch" for Weathertop reference, or keep as exotic fighting style with heavy prereqs.

---

### N:12 Rapid Attack
*Extra attack at -3 Str/Dex*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Swift warriors like Elves. Borderline.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Borderline. Extra attacks push damage output.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Speed vs power trade-off.

**VERDICT: MODIFY** - Rename to "Swift Strikes" and tie to light weapons or one-hand style.

---

### N:13 Strength
*+1 Str*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: N/A - stat boost.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: None.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Capstone reward.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Standard capstone.

---

## Summary Recommendations

### KEEP (No Changes)
- Power (0)
- Finesse (1)
- Knock Back (2)
- Polearm Mastery (3)
- Charge (4)
- Follow-Through (5)
- Subtlety (7)
- Zone of Control (9)
- Strength (13)

### MODIFY
- Impale (6) - Ensure proper weapon gating
- Whirlwind Attack (8) - Reduce scope to 3 enemies max, or require specific weapon
- Smite (10) - Rename "Mighty Blow", change to +Str bonus rather than max damage
- Rapid Attack (12) - Rename "Swift Strikes", tie to light weapons

### REMOVE/REPLACE
- Two Weapon Fighting (11) - No Tolkien precedent. Replace with:
  - **"Sword and Torch"** - Fighting with torch in off-hand grants fire damage and illumination (Weathertop)
  - OR **"Defensive Stance"** - Bonus to evasion when not moving (Helm's Deep wall)

### ADD (Stealth Synergies)
- **"Opening Strike"** - Bonus damage die on first attack against unwary enemy (Rangers)
- **"Fighting Withdrawal"** - Bonus when retreating through enemies (Moria, Osgiliath)
