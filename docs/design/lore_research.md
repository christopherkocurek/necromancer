# Lore Research: Third Age Precedents

## Note: Already Reworked

The Lore tree was converted from "Song" in Alpha 0.1.0 to better fit Third Age. This audit confirms the current design is appropriate.

## Third Age Magic/Knowledge Examples

### Gandalf (Mithrandir)
- **Words of Power**: "Mellon" (friend) to open Moria, "You shall not pass!"
- **Light**: Staff illumination, fireworks
- **Fire**: Lighting pinecones, campfires
- **Breaking spells**: Freeing Theoden from Saruman
- **Device mastery**: Staff use, fireworks

*Techniques implied*: Command words, light magic, fire, counter-magic, device use

### Elrond
- **Healing**: Treating Frodo's morgul wound
- **Lore**: Deep knowledge of history and artifacts
- **Foresight**: Some prescience about the quest

*Techniques implied*: Healing, identification, limited foresight

### Galadriel
- **Mirror**: Showing possible futures
- **Light**: The Phial of Galadriel
- **Perception**: Knowing hearts of visitors
- **Resistance**: Rejecting the Ring's temptation

*Techniques implied*: Divination, light creation, mind reading (limited)

### Aragorn (Healing hands)
- **Athelas**: Knowing herb lore, healing with kingsfoil
- **Command**: "The hands of the king are the hands of a healer"

*Techniques implied*: Herbcraft, royal healing

### Saruman
- **Voice**: Persuasive magic through speech
- **Industry**: Knowledge turned to craft
- **Corruption**: Twisted knowledge

*Techniques implied*: Persuasion magic, device creation

## Lore-Based Abilities in Third Age

### Words of Power
1. **Command words** - Opening doors, commands
2. **Words of binding** - Sealing, shutting
3. **Words of fear** - Commanding enemies
4. **Words of mastery** - Domination (borderline evil)

### Knowledge
1. **Herb lore** - Healing, identification
2. **Deep memory** - Ancestral knowledge
3. **Device mastery** - Using magical items
4. **Secret ways** - Knowing hidden paths

### Light/Sound
1. **Inner light** - Spiritual radiance
2. **Silence** - Dampening sound
3. **Sleep** - Peaceful dreams (Elven)

## Power Ceiling Examples

### Maximum Feats (Third Age cap)
- Gandalf's "You shall not pass" (contest with Balrog)
- Elrond's healing of Frodo
- Galadriel's mirror (showing, not controlling, futures)

### What DOESN'T Happen
- No combat magic (fireballs, lightning bolts)
- No teleportation
- No resurrection
- No mind control (except Saruman's voice, and that's corrupted)
