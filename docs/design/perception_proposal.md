# Perception Proposal: Minimal Changes Needed

## Assessment

The Perception tree is well-designed with strong Tolkien precedents. Only one ability needs modification.

## Proposed Ability Tree

### N:80 Natural Talent (was Quick Study)
**Effect**: Allows you to take advanced abilities without prerequisite abilities
**Level**: 1
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Innate gift, natural aptitude (like Bilbo's unexpected burglary talent)
**Rationale**: RENAMED - "Quick Study" was purely mechanical. "Natural Talent" evokes Tolkien's theme of hidden gifts.

---

### N:81 Focused Attack
**Effect**: +Per/2 attack bonus if you passed the previous turn
**Level**: 2
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Bard's careful aim at Smaug, patient precision
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

**Stealth Hook**: Patience from ambush position

---

### N:82 Keen Senses
**Effect**: See enemies beyond light edge, +5 vs invisible creatures
**Level**: 3
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Elven far-sight, heightened awareness
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

**Stealth Hook**: Detect enemies before they detect you

---

### N:83 Concentration
**Effect**: +1 attack per round vs same enemy (max Per/2)
**Level**: 4
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Studying opponent's patterns
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

---

### N:84 Alchemy
**Effect**: Identify all herbs, potions, staves, and horns
**Level**: 5
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Aragorn's athelas knowledge, Gandalf's lore
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

---

### N:85 Bane
**Effect**: Bonus to all skill rolls vs selected enemy type, increases with kills
**Level**: 6
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Specialized hunters, orc-slayers, dragon-fighters
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

---

### N:86 Outwit
**Effect**: Perception roll vs attacker's Perception to negate critical damage
**Level**: 7
**Prereqs**: None
**Tolkien**: Reading opponent, anticipating attacks
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

---

### N:87 Listen
**Effect**: Chance each turn to detect enemies you cannot see
**Level**: 8
**Prereqs**: Keen Senses
**Tolkien**: Frodo hearing Gollum, Ranger awareness
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

**Stealth Hook**: Know where enemies are to avoid them

---

### N:88 Master Hunter

**Level**: 9
**Prereqs**: Concentration, Bane
**Tolkien**: Learning prey patterns through experience
**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

---

### N:89 Grace
**Effect**: +1 Grace
**Level**: 10
**Prereqs**: None

**Rationale**: UNCHANGED

## Stealth Synergies Summary

1. **Listen**: Know enemy locations to plan route
2. **Keen Senses**: Detect before being detected
3. **Focused Attack**: Patient aim from ambush
4. **Natural Talent**: Quickly access stealth abilities
