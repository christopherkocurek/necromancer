# Layer 6 Vault Design Proposals - The Necromancer
## VAULT-010: Inner Sanctum (Depths 15-18)

---

## Layer Theme Recap
**Setting:** Dark grandeur, Morgul-flame braziers, elite chambers
**Threats:** Black Numenoreans, Olog-hai, Khamul, vampire lords
**Feel:** Final gauntlet, dangerous foes, no room for error
**Key Resistance:** Everything - this is the endgame approach

---

## Vault Design Principles for Layer 6
1. Every enemy is dangerous - no fodder
2. Black Numenoreans combine combat and magic
3. Olog-hai don't fear sunlight - always active
4. Vampire lords are intelligent predators
5. Stealth is still viable but harder
6. Rewards match the difficulty - best loot here

---

## SUMMARY

Layer 6 is the final test before Sauron:
1. **No fodder enemies** - Every fight is meaningful
2. **Elite assassination** - Black Numenoreans as targets
3. **Binding puzzles** - Maia thralls and wards
4. **Darkness combat** - Shadow lords extinguish light
5. **Vampire politics** - Kill lord to scatter thralls
6. **Maximum rewards** - Best treasure for maximum risk
