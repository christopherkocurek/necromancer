# Lore Audit: Current Abilities vs Third Age Precedents

## Note: Already Reworked in Alpha 0.1.0

The Lore tree was converted from "Song" to better fit Third Age. This audit confirms the design.

## Audit Questions Applied to Each Ability

---

### N:140 Word of Command
*Fear nearby enemies*

1. **TOLKIEN PRECEDENT**: Gandalf's commanding voice, words of power.
2. **POWER SCALE**: Grounded. Fear, not destruction.
3. **STEALTH HOOK**: Fear makes enemies flee, easier to avoid.
4. **INTERESTING CHOICE**: Active ability with area effect.

**VERDICT: KEEP** - Good entry-level word of power.

---

## Summary Recommendations

### KEEP (No Changes)
ALL 14 abilities are well-designed for Third Age.

The Lore tree is exemplary:
- Strong Tolkien precedents throughout
- Multiple stealth synergies (Lore of Silence, Lore of Sleep, Word of Shutting)
- Mix of utility, combat, and crowd control
- Clear "wise scholar" fantasy

**Recommendation: No changes needed.**
